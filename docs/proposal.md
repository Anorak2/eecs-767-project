Sample project 1: sentiment analysis
– Data collection: collect tweets on entities of your choice: people, companies, organizations, NFL/NBA/NCAA teams.
– Sentiment analysis: implement a sentiment analysis mechanism to identify the sentiments of the tweets.
– Temporal analysis: to analyze the change of sentiment over time.
– Correlation analysis: to examine the correlation of sentiments toward different entities, e.g., users who are positive with Green Bay Packers are negative with Chicago Bears.
